Privacy Policy

Developer: Dappled Studios ("we", "us")

Effective date: December 31, 2025

Contact: bartek@trailheadgames.com

1) Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Topple Town (the "App") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the App.

2) Information we collect

A. Information you provide

If you contact us for support, we may receive the information you send (for example: your email address, message content, screenshots, and any device/app details you include).

B. Information collected automatically (app + device)

The App and/or third-party SDKs used in the App may automatically collect:

  • Device information (device model, OS version, language)
  • Approximate location inferred from IP address (coarse; not precise GPS)
  • App information (app version, install/source, session timestamps)
  • Advertising identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID / AAID, and similar identifiers)
  • Diagnostics (crash logs, error logs, performance metrics)
  • Engagement/analytics events (e.g., sessions, gameplay progress, ad impressions, rewarded events)

3) How we use information

We use information to:

  • Operate and improve the App (debugging, stability, balancing, performance)
  • Provide customer support
  • Enable advertising (if the App includes ads) and measure ad performance
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and security issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

4) Sharing of information

We may share information with:

  • Service providers / SDK partners that help us run the App (analytics, crash reporting, advertising/mediation, attribution)
  • Legal authorities if required to comply with law, enforce our rights, or protect users and the public

5) Third-party SDKs

Topple Town may use third-party SDKs such as:

  • Ads mediation and ad networks (e.g., ironSource/Supersonic and partners)
  • Attribution/analytics providers (e.g., Meta/Facebook, if enabled)
  • Analytics / crash reporting tools (depending on the build)

These third parties may process information under their own privacy policies.

6) Your choices and controls

You may have options to control data collection:

  • Reset or limit your Advertising ID in your device settings
  • Uninstall the App to stop further collection from that device
  • Where required, consent choices may be provided for personalized advertising

7) Data retention

  • We retain support communications as long as needed to address your request and keep reasonable records.
  • Data collected by third-party SDK providers is retained according to those providers' policies unless deleted/anonymized through a request (see below).

8) Data Deletion Request

You can request deletion of personal data associated with your use of the App.

How to request deletion:

Email bartek@trailheadgames.com with the subject line: "Topple Town Data Deletion Request" and include:

  • Your device platform (Android/iOS)
  • Your approximate install date (if known)
  • Your Advertising ID (AAID/IDFA) if you can access it, or any other identifier/details that help us locate your data (for example: support email used, approximate last played date)

What we do:

  • We will delete or anonymize data we control that we can reasonably identify.
  • If some data is processed by third-party SDK providers (ads/analytics), we will take reasonable steps to request deletion/anonymization where applicable, and/or provide guidance on any provider-specific steps if needed.

9) Children's privacy

Topple Town is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us at bartek@trailheadgames.com and we will take steps to delete it.

10) International users

If you use the App outside Canada, your information may be processed in other countries where we or our service providers operate.

11) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version at this URL and update the "Effective date."

12) Contact

For questions, requests, or concerns, email: bartek@trailheadgames.com
